§ 01 — /compress

Trim without leaving.

Type /compress in any session. The hook writes a trimmed sibling with a fresh UUID, prints what you saved, and tells you what to resume. Six modes from surgical to nuclear. Original JSONL untouched.

For seamless exits, ccw wraps claude and auto-resumes into the trimmed session on Ctrl+C.

┌─ claudecompress ────────────────────────────────┐ mode: focus (last 20) · dropped thinking tokens: 628.8k → 126.0k (saved ≈ 502.8k) cold $ $9.43 → $1.89 (saved ≈ $7.54) trimmed: 17420d99-7152-4359-bfdd… └────────────────────────────────────────────────┘ Ctrl+C, then: claude --resume 17420d99…
§ 02 — offline trimmer

One command,
any session.

For sessions you didn't trim in the moment. The picker auto-detects your project, flags cache staleness, shows cost per-session, writes a new JSONL alongside the old.

$ bunx claudecompress # lists your sessions, staleness, cost # pick a mode, confirm # new trimmed JSONL written alongside

§ 04 — install

Install. Restart.
Done.

Global install required — the status line ticks once per second and needs fast startup. Bun is preferred; node works fine. Installer backs up your settings.json and never overwrites anything without asking.

$ bun add -g claudecompress # or: npm i -g claudecompress $ claudecompress install # pick: hook, status line, or both # fully quit & relaunch Claude Code. done.
